Hockey Hub - Quick portal for Hockey nerds

Just type a command and hit go.

Commands

!draft year Draft year on HockeyDB
!draft team Team draft history on HockeyDB
!cap team Team cap on CapFriendly
!cap player Player contract on CapFriendly
!depth team Depth chart on Elite Prospects
!prospects team Prospects (in the system) on Elite Prospects
!trades team Trade history on NHL Trade Tracker
!reddit team Team subreddit on reddit

Teams

Teams can be referred to by code, location or names. The field is not case sensitive. For instance the Montreal Canadiens can be referred to as mtl, montreal, canadiens or habs.
